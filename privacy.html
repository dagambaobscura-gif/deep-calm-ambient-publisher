Privacy Policy

Effective July 15, 2026

This policy explains how Deep Calm Ambient Publisher handles information when the channel owner connects a TikTok account and publishes channel content.

Information we process

The service may receive basic TikTok account information, authorization tokens, selected video files, captions, privacy settings, and publishing status. It does not request TikTok passwords.

How information is used

Information is used only to authenticate the channel owner, prepare requested posts, transfer selected videos to TikTok, and display publishing results.

Storage and sharing

Selected videos and account data are not sold or used for advertising. Information is shared with TikTok only as required to complete an authorized publishing action.

Data deletion

The channel owner can revoke access in TikTok account settings. Requests concerning stored publishing data can be sent to dagambaobscura@gmail.com.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the publishing workflow or platform requirements change.
